Weather Preparedness Tips

Gear up for any weather twist with must-know weather preparedness tips! Whether rain or shine, stay ahead of the forecast and safeguard yourself with these essential strategies.

Storm Safety Tips

  • Stay indoors and away from windows during thunderstorms.
  • Unplug electronic devices to prevent damage from power surges.
  • Avoid using landline phones and plumbing during lightning storms.
  • Secure outdoor furniture and objects that could become projectiles in strong winds.
  • Listen to weather updates and follow instructions from local authorities.

Extreme Weather Tips

  • Always carry Emergency kit in your car.
  • It is important to prepare your yard in the case of severe weather.
  • Check on elderly and those with medical conditions during temperature extremes.
  • Keep pets indoors and ensure they have access to water and shelter.
  • Use caution when using heating devices to prevent fire hazards.

Natural Disaster Tips

  • Know the evacuation routes in your area and have a designated meeting point for family members.
  • Keep an emergency kit stocked with water, non-perishable food, flashlights, batteries, and first aid supplies.
  • Learn basic first aid and CPR techniques.
  • Practice earthquake drills with your family and know how to "Drop, Cover, and Hold On".
  • Follow orders from authorities during floods, wildfires, or other natural disasters.

Emergency Helpline Numbers

  • National Emergency Number: 112
  • Ambulance: 102
  • Disaster Management Services: 108
  • Earthquake/Flood/Disaster: 011-24363260
